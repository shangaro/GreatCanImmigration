ABOUT US

GreatCan Immigration Services Inc. is a leading Canadian immigration services firm that provides immigration services for people looking to come to Canada.

Why choose us?

Our Immigration Consultants guide you on ways you can come to Canada. Our consultants are fully authorized as Registered Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) and are active members with the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC).

An experienced RCIC can guide you with ways to achieve your immigration goal.Since RCIC’s membership is governed by immigration laws, RCICs are obliged to abide by professional conduct and these are very similar to that of lawyers conduct.

Reasons to Hire us

Registered Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) are members in good standing with Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC). ICCRC is a regulatory body authorized by Canada immigration rules and regulations that regulates Immigration and citizenship consultants and international student’s advisors. Members of ICCRC can advise the public on Canadian immigration matters.

Since RCIC’s membership is governed by immigration laws, RCICs are obliged to abide by professional conduct and these are very similar to that of lawyers conduct.
